KioskMeet is a web application that aims to be the one stop solution for class-wise information management. Students can track their courses, schedule and attendance as well as check their disciplinary action and marks among others.As an additional feature, KioskMeet developers plan to remove external dependency for online classes by integrating a video calling application inside the portal itself.
